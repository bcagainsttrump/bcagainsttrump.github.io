NO BAN, NO WALL: The Struggle for Immigrant Rights Under Trump


led by Prof. Mojúbàolú Olufúnké Okome



A panel discussion featuring Sami Disu, independent journalist, Luiza Tanuri of Make the Road, and Yasmine Kamel of Bay Ridge for Social Justice. Activists will speak about their resistance to the Trump administration's xenophobic and Islamophobic policies and actions and why it's important to engage this struggle today. - We Stand Against Hate
